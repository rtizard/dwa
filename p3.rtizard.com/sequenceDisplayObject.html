Basic Protein Sequence Analysis

  1. Start by entering a sequence below and tabbing out of the area. Editing by hand also works. Spaces and numbers will be removed.
  2. Don't have a sequence handy? Click here for human MIS demo sequence.
  3. Your formatted sequence will appear on the right. The chain length, molecular weight, isoelectric point, charge at pH 7, and molar extinction coefficient will be displayed below.
  4. Click on one of the 20 amino acid residue buttons to count their appearances in your sequence and highlight them.
  5. The color intensity of the 20 amino acid buttons encodes a value. Only the incidence count depends on your input sequence.
  6. The four possible encoded values are selected from the radio buttons. I'm working out a method to reveal all the properties stored for each amino acid on either click or hover. Thoughts?
  7. Layout change suggestions are welcome. I'm still working on the quiz feature.
