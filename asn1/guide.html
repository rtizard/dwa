User Guide

  1. Your handheld must be in landscape orientation. From portrait mode, rotate counterclockwise.
  2. The red ball is released by touching the Release Ball button.
  3. Three balls per game.
  4. 50 points for hitting the green target, which also ends the game.
  5. 25 points for hitting a pot of gold.
  6. 10 points are lost for hitting a banana peel, which also can result in a nasty spill.
  7. Hitting a bomb ends the current ball without a point penalty.
  8. The five highest scores for the device are stored anonymously.
